When the Sea tells you a story..

We create stories of the sea, people, and nature. Our collections are produced on a small scale, with some crafted from recycled Italian fabrics. We take our time with every stitch, ensuring that each bikini is perfectly made by hand, a reflection of care, patience, and dedication.

Artisan Craftsmanship

Every piece is carefully made in a single artisan factory in Bali, where local makers are valued, respected, and paid fairly for their craft.

Salty Shells Project

Through our Salty Shells Project, we are proud to collaborate with the Turtle Conservation Foundation in Indonesia YPI (https://yayasanpenyu.org/), giving back to the ocean that inspires us. A portion of every purchase supports the protection of sea turtles and their natural habitats, ensuring their survival for generations to come.

For us, every piece you wear carries a real impact for the ocean, the earth, and the community behind it.
